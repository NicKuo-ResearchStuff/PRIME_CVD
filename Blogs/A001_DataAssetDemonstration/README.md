## Data Asset 1 & 2 — Blog Overview

<img src="https://github.com/NicKuo-ResearchStuff/PRIME_CVD/blob/main/Supporting_Images/ZFig_PRIME_CVD_Series01_Logo.png" width="600"/>

* **Data Asset 1: A Clean, Causal Cohort for Teaching Cardiovascular Risk Modelling** (see [here](https://github.com/NicKuo-ResearchStuff/PRIME_CVD/tree/main/Blogs/A001_DataAssetDemonstration/SubBlog01))</br>
  → A fully simulated, analysis-ready 50,000-person cohort built to safely teach cardiovascular risk modelling and causal reasoning.

* **Data Asset 1: Synthetic Cohort Characteristics and Baseline Structure** (see [here](https://github.com/NicKuo-ResearchStuff/PRIME_CVD/tree/main/Blogs/A001_DataAssetDemonstration/SubBlog02))</br>
  → A formal documentation summary of the cohort’s distributions, structure, and outcome profile for citation and reproducible use.

* **Data Asset 2: A Relational EMR-Style Dataset for Teaching Data Cleaning and Reconstruction** (see [here](https://github.com/NicKuo-ResearchStuff/PRIME_CVD/tree/main/Blogs/A001_DataAssetDemonstration/SubBlog03))</br>
  → A deliberately fragmented synthetic EMR dataset designed to teach cleaning, linkage, and cohort reconstruction before modelling.

* **Data Asset 1: Where Did This Dataset Come From?** (see [here](https://github.com/NicKuo-ResearchStuff/PRIME_CVD/tree/main/Blogs/A001_DataAssetDemonstration/SubBlog04))</br>
  → A transparent explanation of how the cohort was constructed from a hand-specified causal DAG and public Australian evidence.

* **Data Asset 1: Designing a Simulated Cohort from Public Evidence (Smoking Conditional on IRSD Example)** (see [here](https://github.com/NicKuo-ResearchStuff/PRIME_CVD/tree/main/Blogs/A001_DataAssetDemonstration/SubBlog05))</br>
  → A worked generative example demonstrating how public evidence is translated into explicit probabilistic assumptions within the synthetic cohort.
